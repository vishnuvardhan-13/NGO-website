Who are we?

We are Helping Heroes

We are HHIF, an organization dedicated to alleviating hunger and empowering underserved communities. Since 2020, we’ve provided nutritious meals to over 2,000 people daily in South Bengaluru. Beyond food, we support skill development by distributing sewing machines and offering educational resources, including scholarships. Our mission is to foster self-sufficiency and dignity, helping individuals break the cycle of poverty and lead fulfilling lives.
